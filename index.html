Hopscotch to Infinity

Radmila Sazdanović

Art inspired by the interplay of nature, culture, and everyday life with the symmetries of tessellations of hyperbolic, negatively curved space. These tessellations, appearing in art and architecture as well as in mathematics, are created using photography and computer software, such as Tess and KnotPlot, and aspire to convey the beauty and subtlety of mathematical ideas without any prior knowledge.

Learn more about Radmila's work at http://www4.ncsu.edu/~rsazdan/.
